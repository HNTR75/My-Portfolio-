About Me

I've been interested in design and technology since I was a child. I remember always being fascinated by the way that products could be used to solve problems and make people's lives easier. As I got older, I started to learn more about the design process and how to use technology to create user-centered products.

I'm currently a student at the Malawi University of Business and Applied Sciences, where I'm studying Management Information Systems. I'm also an upcoming Data Analyst, and I'm excited to learn more about how data can be used to improve the way that products are designed and developed.

In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my family and friends, playing soccer, and reading. I'm also a big fan of design blogs and podcasts, and I'm always looking for new ways to learn and grow as a designer.

I'm passionate about creating user-centered products that make people's lives easier. I believe that good design can make a real difference in the world, and I'm excited to use my skills to make a positive impact.

I'm always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow. If you have a project that you think I would be a good fit for, please don't hesitate to reach out. I'd love to hear from you!
